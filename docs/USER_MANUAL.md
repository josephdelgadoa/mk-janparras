## 2. Smart Articles (Content Engine)
*Stop writing blog posts from scratch. Let AI do the heavy lifting.*

### How to Generate an Article
1.  Navigate to **"Smart Articles"** in the sidebar.
2.  **Topic Input:** Enter a keyword or topic (e.g., "Bohemian Beach Weddings in Puerto Vallarta").
3.  **Generate Draft:** Click "Generate". The AI researches low-competition keywords, structures the post, and writes a full draft optimized for SEO.
    *   *Note: This process may take 30-60 seconds.*
4.  **Review & Polish:** The system presents a clean editor.
    *   Read through the content.
    *   Adjust the tone or specific details if needed.
5.  **Publish:** Click **"Publish to WordPress"**. The article is instantly live on your website's blog, driving organic traffic.

**Best Practice:** aim to publish 2-3 articles per week to maintain "Search Engine Dominance".

## 3. Brand Ambassador Toolkit
*Be everywhere, all at once.*

This module allows you to create marketing assets featuring "Robin" (the brand face) without needing a photoshoot.

### Generating Assets
1.  Navigate to **"Brand Ambassador"**.
2.  **Select Scenario:** Choose a setting (e.g., "Golden Hour Beach", "Luxury Villa Interaction", "Office Planning").
3.  **Generate Avatar:** The AI creates a photorealistic image of Robin in that setting.
4.  **Download:** Save the image for use in Instagram stories, Facebook ads, or email headers.

### Voice Scripts
1.  Select **"Voice Scripts"**.
2.  Choose a **Topic** (e.g., "New Venue Alert") and **Tone** (e.g., "Excited", "Professional").
3.  **Generate:** You get a 30-second script perfect for Instagram Reels or TikToks.

## 4. Email Marketing Studio
*Nurture leads while you sleep.*

### Managing Templates
1.  Navigate to **"Email Marketing"**.
2.  **Template Gallery:** View your active templates (e.g., "Welcome", "Venues Recommendation").
3.  **Edit:** Click "Edit" on any template card.
    *   **Visual Editor:** Modify the text, change images, and update links.
    *   **Live Preview:** See exactly how it will look on mobile/desktop.
4.  **Save:** Changes are instantly applied to all future automated emails.
5.  **Test:** Use the "Send Test" feature to email yourself a copy before saving.

**Key Templates:**
*   **Welcome Email:** Sent immediately upon inquiry.
*   **Venues Recommendation:** A visual guide to top locations.
*   **Beach Wedding & Vow Renewals:** Package details.
